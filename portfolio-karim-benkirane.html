About

Engineering Intelligence,
One System at a Time

I'm an AI & Data Engineer with a Master's degree (Expert Chef de Projet IA & Data) from EPSI Paris, specializing in the full spectrum from raw data pipelines to production-grade machine learning and generative AI systems.

Currently on an apprenticeship as Innovation & AI Project Manager at Kisio (Keolis Group), I lead the design and deployment of AI solutions applied to intelligent mobility — including a RAG-based conversational assistant for operational procedures, autonomous capsule incident supervision, and large-scale data industrialization pipelines using Python, Pandas, GTFS datasets, and Agile/Scrum methodologies.

My technical focus spans Generative AI architectures (LangChain, FAISS, SentenceTransformers), predictive ML systems (TensorFlow, Scikit-learn), data engineering (Apache Airflow, ETL pipelines, PostgreSQL), and backend development with FastAPI and Flask. I'm equally comfortable at the whiteboard architecting a system and in the terminal shipping it.

I believe the most powerful AI systems are those built with rigorous engineering discipline — clean pipelines, robust validation, and measurable outcomes. Every project I ship is held to production standards, not just proof-of-concept quality.

FAISS vector index for sub-second similarity search

FastAPI REST backend + streaming response support
